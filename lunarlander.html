Lunar Lander



Production: December 2022 - April 2023 || Platform: Unity || Singleplayer, Mobile Game


Lunar Lander is a mobile game prototype created by h_da students in cooperation with the European Space Agency (ESA). The project was created in an effort to train an AI to identify moon crators as precisely as possible for a lunar mission planned in the future. We aimed to gamify this process.
