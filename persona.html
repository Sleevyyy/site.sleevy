WHO AM I?

My name is Emely , I am 23 years old and I currently study Animation and Game at Hochschule Darmstadt in my 6th semester.

I have been studying since fall 2021 and in summer 2025, I will be done with my bachelor thesis.

My love and passion for games started, when I was a child, playing all of the classics, mostly from nintendo. Over the years, my taste in games developed further into liking more and more small, passionate indie games. It all started out with Pinstripe, followed by Hollow Knight, Spiritfarer (which had me crying 80% of the time), Cult of the Lamb, Ooblets, Minute of Islands and so on! You could say I love the wonky-stylized aesthetic.

My goal is to work in the indie-game industry at some point in my life! I love the diversity, the possibilities and a small teamsize, so I can get to know all my co-workers names and their silly little interests. And to crochet them a bunch of figures they don't really need! (Ever!)

Next to that, I am a plant and cat mom, and I love visiting hardcore and beatdown concerts :)
