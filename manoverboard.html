Man Overboard!
Local Multiplayer, Couch-Coop Platform: Unreal Engine

"You and your crew just found the most dazzling pirate treasure imaginable, now you just have to bring it to your harbor back home. Problem is just: A huge ship like that needs intense cooperation from the crew and . A whacky and chaotic game in which you have to reach the harbor safely with as little casualties and as much treasure as possible. Constantly keep an eye on occuring problems on the ship,delete fires, save each other from the kraken or defend them from other pirates and bring your treasure home!"





Working on this project in a team of 5 from October 2023 until February 2024, I worked on this project as a Game Designer and part-time Technical Artist.
I was mainly responsible for creating the core game mechanic and different events which should help to make the game more dynamic and for the Animation/-Sound implementation of the characters, as well as for half of the character creation.
