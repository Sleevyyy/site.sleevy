The mighty Egg-watch!
Multiplayer, Capture-the-Flag Platform: Unity

Since the dawn of time itself, the "mighty Egg-Watch" has governed the garden's rhythm, providing sanctuary to caterpillars from the looming presence of the human-gods, as long as its timer ticks. Fueled by a longing for culinary excellence, these creatures engage in a fierce competition to craft the ultimate salad before the final grains of sand slip away. Amidst this saga of rivalry and cunning, only one caterpillar will ascend to triumph by earning the highest rating for their salad. Now, it's your turn to strategize, sabotage, and strive for victory in this whimsical garden battleground.

For this project I am working as the Game Designer and Co-Programmer.
