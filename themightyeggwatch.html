The Mighty Eggwatch



Production: April 2024 - July 2024 || Platform: Unity || Multiplayer, 3D top-down, Party-Game



“The Mighty Egg Watch” is a 3D top-down multiplayer party-game in which each caterpillar has to collect the ingredients for the best salad they have ever had. Flinging themselves on top of carefully attended raised beds, ripping out the best looking vegetables or eating them directly and discovering all the effects they have on them, without running out of time or having their salad ruined by the wrong ingredients, keeps the players engaged in this competitive and interactive party-game. Who will have the best salad in the end? Who will try to ruin someone else’s salad with the wrong ingredients? Who will munch on all the tasty veggies and steal them from others? All is up to you!
