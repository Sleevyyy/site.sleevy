Floral Frenzy



"Welcome to Petalpit"

Singleplayer, Strategy-Game Platform: Unity

Stranded in a mysterious village whose residents seem nice at first, but make living there dangerous and scary, you realize you have to find a way out of there. Through cultivating flowers you can gain the trust of the villagers who might be able to help you repair the trainstation destroyed in an explosion.






Working on this project in a team of 4 from October 2022 until February 2023, I worked on this project as a Game Designer and part-time Technical Artist.
I was responsible for creating the mechanics and for creating some of the lore and character designs of the game.

In the beginning, we tested a lot with different approaches and worked with a paper prototype to test out different chances of villagers attacking and under which conditions.
